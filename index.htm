Dr. Pratima Mittal, FRCOG (Hons)

Professor, Consultant & Head of Department, Obstetrics & Gynaecology
Amrita Institute Of Medical Sciences, Faridabad

About

Dr. Pratima Mittal is a highly distinguished obstetrician and gynaecologist with over 38 years of experience. She has held prestigious academic and leadership positions including HOD at Safdarjung Hospital and WHO Consultant for multiple maternal health initiatives. Her expertise lies in high-risk pregnancy, adolescent health, and critical care obstetrics.

Education

  • MBBS, Lady Hardinge Medical College, Delhi (1977)
  • MD (Obs & Gynae), Maulana Azad Medical College, Delhi (1982)
  • PG Diploma in Health & Hospital Management, IGNOU (2002)
  • FRCOG (Hon.), Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists, London (2019)

Key Achievements

  • Founded India’s first dedicated Critical Care Unit for Obstetric patients at Safdarjung Hospital
  • Developed national training manuals and guidelines for maternal health under the MOHFW
  • Led WHO-backed projects on preterm birth, stillbirth registry, adolescent health, and more
  • Published over 70 research papers and 20+ book chapters

Collaborations

Actively involved with WHO, ICMR, DBT, and several international institutions like Liverpool School of Tropical Medicine. Served as expert advisor for UPSC, GOI policy development, and academic councils.

Professional Societies

  • Vice President, FOGSI (2018)
  • President, AOGD (2015-16)
  • Executive member, Adolescent Health Committee FOGSI (2003-2017)

Public Engagement

Conducted numerous health awareness camps, TV/radio programs, and public lectures on maternal and reproductive health. Organized youth melas and training programs for adolescent care across India.
