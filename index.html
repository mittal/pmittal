About Me

Dr. Pratima Mittal is a highly accomplished Professor (Emeritus) of Obstetrics and Gynecology with 38+ years of experience in clinical practice, academic leadership, and public health initiatives. A WHO Consultant and recognized expert in high-risk pregnancy and adolescent health, she holds an MD and FRCOG (Hons) and is eligible to work in the US without visa sponsorship. She is seeking to contribute her expertise to a challenging role in an academic or public health setting in the United States.
